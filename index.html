First Tawheed School

Established in 2015, First Tawheed School has been a beacon of Islamic education combined with modern academic excellence. Our mission is to nurture well-rounded individuals who excel both spiritually and intellectually.

With over 100 alumni across various disciplines, we continue to innovate our teaching methods to prepare students for the challenges of the 21st century while upholding fundamental Islamic values.
